# Contributing Feedback

Thank you for helping improve HiveMinded.ai.

## Choose the right form

Use the structured issue forms for bugs, QA findings, accessibility problems, and feature requests. Please do not use public issues for security vulnerabilities or sensitive account-specific support.

## Write a useful report

A strong report includes:

1. A specific title.
2. The page, feature, or workflow affected.
3. Exact steps to reproduce the problem.
4. Expected and actual behavior.
5. Browser, operating system, and device details.
6. Screenshots or recordings with sensitive data removed.
7. How frequently the issue occurs and its impact.

## Public information only

Everything submitted here may be visible publicly. Remove customer names, tenant data, email addresses, phone numbers, credentials, tokens, financial data, health data, and proprietary information.

## Triage process

Maintainers may request additional information, close duplicates, or transfer a sanitized summary into a private engineering backlog. Acceptance does not guarantee a release date. Please use reactions instead of posting repeated `+1` comments.

## Conduct

Participation is governed by [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md).
