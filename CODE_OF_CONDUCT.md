# Community Code of Conduct

We want this feedback space to be welcoming, useful, and professional.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Focus criticism on the product or process, not individuals.
- Provide enough detail for others to understand the issue.
- Respect privacy and remove sensitive information.
- Follow maintainer requests during triage.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, personal attacks, spam, intentional disclosure of private information, and abusive or disruptive conduct are not permitted.

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove content and may restrict participation when necessary to protect the community and the project.

Report conduct concerns privately through the official contact method at https://saasysolutionsllc.com.
