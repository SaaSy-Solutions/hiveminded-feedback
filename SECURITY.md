# Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Do **not** open a public issue for suspected security vulnerabilities.

Use GitHub Private Vulnerability Reporting when it is enabled for this repository. If that option is unavailable, contact SaaSy Solutions through the official contact method listed at https://saasysolutionsllc.com and clearly mark the message as a security report.

Include:

- The affected product area and URL.
- A clear description of the vulnerability.
- Reproduction steps or a minimal proof of concept.
- The potential impact.
- Any suggested mitigation.
- Your preferred contact information.

Do not include real customer data, access other users' information, disrupt service, perform denial-of-service testing, use social engineering, or retain sensitive information discovered during testing.

We will review good-faith reports and coordinate follow-up when contact information is available. Do not publicly disclose a vulnerability before SaaSy Solutions has had a reasonable opportunity to investigate and remediate it.
